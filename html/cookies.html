Cookie Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how Beas Delicacies uses cookies and similar tracking technologies when you visit our website. By using our website, you consent to our use of cookies as described in this policy.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and improve your browsing experience.

Cookies can be:

  • Session Cookies: Temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser
  • Persistent Cookies: Cookies that remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them
  • First-Party Cookies: Cookies set by our website
  • Third-Party Cookies: Cookies set by external services we use

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for several purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function properly (e.g., shopping cart, login sessions)
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our website to improve functionality
  • Functionality Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analytics Cookies: Track website traffic and user behavior

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Shopping Cart Stores items you add to your cart Session
User Session Maintains your login status Session
User Preferences Remembers your settings and preferences 1 year
Analytics Tracks website usage and visitor behavior 2 years
Newsletter Subscription Stores newsletter subscription status Persistent

4. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services that set cookies on your device, including:

  • Payment Processors: Stripe and PayPal for secure payment processing
  • Social Media: Cookies from social media platforms when you interact with social sharing buttons
  • Analytics Services: Tools that help us analyze website traffic and user behavior

These third-party cookies are governed by the respective privacy policies of these external services.

5. Local Storage

In addition to cookies, we use browser local storage to:

  • Store shopping cart contents
  • Save newsletter subscription preferences
  • Remember user authentication state

Local storage data remains on your device until you clear it through your browser settings.

6. Managing Cookies

You have the right to accept or reject cookies. Here's how you can manage cookies:

Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can:

  • Block all cookies
  • Block third-party cookies only
  • Delete cookies after each browsing session
  • Accept or reject cookies on a case-by-case basis

Browser-Specific Instructions:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Cookies and website data
  • Edge: Settings > Cookies and site permissions > Cookies and site data

Important Note: If you block or delete essential cookies, some features of our website may not function properly, and you may not be able to:

  • Add items to your shopping cart
  • Complete purchases
  • Stay logged into your account
  • Access certain features or pages

7. Do Not Track Signals

Some browsers include a "Do Not Track" (DNT) feature that signals websites you visit that you do not want to be tracked. Currently, there is no universally accepted standard for how to respond to DNT signals. We do not currently respond to DNT signals, but we respect your privacy choices and provide cookie management options as described above.

8. Cookie Consent

By continuing to use our website without changing your cookie settings, you consent to our use of cookies as described in this policy. You can withdraw your consent at any time by adjusting your browser settings.

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, regulatory, or operational reasons. We will post any updates on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.
